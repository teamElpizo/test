Manning365

This app creates a user interface for first responders who also hold the secondary position of Roster-in-charge, to create, manage and maintain the schedules of their fellow colleagues.

This solutions aims to address the problems explained explained here, and is one application Team Elpizo has coded as part of our integrated solutions.

Manning365 has many functionalities, allowing the roster IC to:

  • Add/Remove new personnel from the system
  • Add/Edit the personnel's particulars in the system
  • Generate a unique schedule for everybody, as well as choose the format to display the roster- by Rota, role or rank
  • Generate an individual specs sheet detailing the number of MCs taken, when taken and how much left for the year
  • Generate an individual specs sheet detailing the number of VLs taken, when taken and how much left for the year
  • Generate an individual specs sheet detailing the personnel's particulars, shift pattern for the week/month, call sign attached to previously
  • Update the manning and vechicles personnel is attached to for previous shifts

However, do note that applying for VLs, OVLs permission, checking of payslip and submission of reports will still be done via the HRMS, NSpam and DMGX. We can add this application to the intranet system that is already in place in SCDF, or provide a secure password protected and encrypted platform for home access.
